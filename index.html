The Profile

About Me

I'm a sports journalist and graduate student at Northwestern University's Medill School of Journalism. My work focuses on the intersection of sports, community, human rights, and culture.

I'm drawn to stories that explore how sports reflect and shape our communities—from grassroots organizing in women's leagues to the complex fan discourse that follows.
